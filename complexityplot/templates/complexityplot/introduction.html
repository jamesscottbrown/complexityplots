To accompany our Eurovis 2013 paper, we have developed a web-enabled implementation of our complexity plot. Our online tool allows you to upload your own input size versus measurement (e.g. algorithm runtime) .csv files to be analysed by our complexity estimator and plotted onto a complexity plot. This tool is a work-in-progress, and all feedback is very welcome.

We have a brief guide to the complexity plot available if you don't have time to read our paper. Otherwise, you can upload your own measurements data or view a gallery of user-submitted visualizations below. Have fun!
